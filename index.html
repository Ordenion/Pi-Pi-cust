Discover Rotterdam

Rotterdam is a vibrant and modern city known for its unique architecture, rich culture, and dynamic port. Here are some top places to visit during your stay:

1. Erasmus Bridge

The iconic Erasmus Bridge, also known as "The Swan," is a symbol of Rotterdamâ€™s modernity. Its sleek design and stunning views of the cityâ€™s skyline make it a must-see attraction.

2. Markthal

This impressive indoor market hall is not just a place to enjoy delicious food but also a work of art. The ceiling of the Markthal is adorned with the world's largest artwork, making it a feast for both your eyes and taste buds.

3. Cube Houses

The Cube Houses are a perfect example of Rotterdam's innovative architectural design. These tilted cube-shaped buildings are fascinating to explore and offer a unique view of urban living.

4. Kunsthal Rotterdam

Kunsthal Rotterdam is a unique and innovative museum that hosts a wide range of temporary exhibitions. From classic art to contemporary designs, photography, and fashion, the museum offers an eclectic mix of cultural experiences, making it a top destination for art lovers.

5. Euromast Tower

Standing at 185 meters, the Euromast Tower offers breathtaking panoramic views of Rotterdam's skyline. Visitors can dine in the restaurant located at the top or enjoy a thrilling abseiling experience for an unforgettable adventure. It's a perfect spot to get a 360-degree view of the city.
